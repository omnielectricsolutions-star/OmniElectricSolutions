About OmniElectric Solution

OmniElectrics Solution is a specialized engineering firm offering expert services in electrical power system analysis and renewable energy design. With over 7 years of industry experience, our company is driven by a highly skilled team of engineers who bring deep technical expertise and a strong foundation in electrical engineering.

In addition to power systems analysis, OmniElectrics Solution also provides comprehensive technical reporting services and solar PV system design using PVsyst, tailored to meet the unique needs of utility, commercial, and industrial clients.

Our engineers are not only certified and experienced, but also committed to delivering reliable, efficient, and standards-compliant solutions to complex power system challenges. At OmniElectrics Solution, we pride ourselves on our collaborative approach, technical excellence, and a results-driven mindset.
